About The Event

Sulakshya 2022, the 8th edition of the annual cultural fest, brings to students of classes 6, 7 and 8, a stage perfectly lit up to celebrate their talents. the exciting two-day event comprising fun-filled competitions like dance, music, quiz, photography and so many more, is all set to welcome you. an array of enthralling experience and compelling prizes are in store for you. the cultural extravaganza eagerly awaits your enthusiastic participation!
